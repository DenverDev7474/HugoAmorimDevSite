About

As a full-stack developer, I am passionate about finding intuitive solutions to difficult problems. I think that the best technology is simple and has the power to change the way we do things. My background in design allows me to approach programming in a creative way, always considering the best user experience. I have honed my skills in learning and adding new programming languages to my toolkit, and I am always excited to learn more.

I've had the pleasure of learning from great institutions like Le Wagon, where I completed an immersive Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, diving deep into languages like Ruby and JavaScript. At General Assembly, I honed my UX design skills, mastering tools like Sketch and Invision to create user-friendly designs. Most recently, I've been a part of EY's Product Management Academy, where I picked up agile methodologies, scrum, and product development strategies to manage products effectively. Each of these experiences has shaped me into the versatile and growth-oriented developer I am today.
